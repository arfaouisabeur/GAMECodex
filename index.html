Who We Are

CODX is an online learning platform that provides an innovative way of learning while having fun.

Users of CODX will be able to learn and practice problem solving in a competitive and fun environement uning our multiplayer Tick Tack Toe game.

What We Do

CODX platform provides a very simple yet highly efficient learning method.
Consisting of only 4 easy steps:

1- Enter

2- Play

3- Learn

4- Repeat

Practice

Providing you with endless problems to solve, you'll always find something new to look into, especially when taking in consideration the increasing difficulty of these challenges as your profile goes up in rank

Competition

The icing on the cake, having not only to finish the challenge, but to also to be ahead of your opponent, who's willing to do his best in order to win those strategic blocks you want, to stop you from winning, and claim victory for themselves.
